About

Falaises d'Étretat

A source of inspiration for artists such as Boudin and Monet, these white limestone cliffs form a dazzling and spectacular setting.

Claude Monet's House and Gardens in Givernyque faire en normandie?

Having inspired many of Monet's paintings, these gardens are typical of Normandy, with their climbing plants and water lily ponds.

Rouen Cathedral

This impressive Gothic cathedral is one of Rouen's main attractions and was the main subject of many of Monet's paintings.

Visit Strasbourg, capital of Alsace

Strasbourg will charm you with its half-timbered houses, canals and bridges. It is in this romantic city that the European Parliament is located.

Colmar

Considered the wine capital of Alsace, Colmar is built on the Lauch, which gives it the name of Little Venice.

Colmar

Mulhouse will introduce you to its medieval houses with colorful facades, its Saint-Étienne church with 14th century stained glass windows and its Cité de l’Automobile..
